# Sign-Rank, Index, and List Replicability: Connections and Separations

> ArXiv | 2026-06-17 | paper 7

In learning theory, the sign rank of a binary concept class captures the smallest dimension in which it can be represented by points and halfspaces. Despite tremendous interest, lower bounds on sign rank are notoriously difficult to come by.
