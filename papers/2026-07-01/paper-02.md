# GEAR: Guided End-to-End AutoRegression for Image Synthesis

> ArXiv | 2026-07-01 | paper 2

Visual generative models are typically trained in two stages. A tokenizer is first trained for reconstruction and then frozen, after which a generator is trained on its discrete indices or continuous latents.
