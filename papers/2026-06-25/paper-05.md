# MVTrack4Gen: Multi-View Point Tracking as Geometric Supervision for 4D Video Generation

> ArXiv | 2026-06-25 | paper 5

Synthesizing a novel-view video from a monocular reference video along a target camera trajectory requires both geometric consistency and motion fidelity with respect to the reference video. Existing methods based on explicit 3D representations are limited by the accuracy of off-the-shelf reconstruction modules, which often produce inaccurate geometry for dynamic objects in monocular videos.
