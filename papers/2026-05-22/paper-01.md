# Tokenisation via Convex Relaxations

> ArXiv | 2026-05-22 | paper 1

Tokenisation is an integral part of the current NLP pipeline. Current tokenisation algorithms such as BPE and Unigram are greedy algorithms -- they make locally optimal decisions without considering the resulting vocabulary as a whole.
