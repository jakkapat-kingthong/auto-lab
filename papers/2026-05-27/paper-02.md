# G3T Up! Gravity Aligned Coordinate Frames Simplify Pointmap Processing

> ArXiv | 2026-05-27 | paper 2

Modern feed-forward 3D reconstruction methods like VGGT predict pixel-aligned pointmaps in camera-centric coordinate frames. However, this choice of coordinate frame is not always optimal. We propose instead to predict pointmaps in upright, gravity-aligned frames that exploit strong structural cues present in many real-world scenes.
