# Integrable Elasticity via Neural Demand Potentials

> ArXiv | 2026-05-23 | paper 3

We propose the Integrable Context-Dependent Demand Network (ICDN), a demand-first neural model for multiproduct retail demand. The model learns log-demand as a smooth, context-conditioned function of log-prices, allowing elasticities to be derived exactly from the learned demand surface.
